About Us

Welcome to Ayansh International Trading

Welcome to Ayansh International Trading FZ-LLC, your trusted partner for fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) in the heart of Dubai, UAE. We specialize in supplying high-quality products to various African countries including Rwanda, Uganda, DR Congo, South Sudan, Central African Republic, Burundi, Kenya, Tanzania, and many more.

At Ayansh International Trading, we specialize in sourcing and distributing top-notch FMCG products across various categories including food and beverages, Wines & Spirits and more. With a keen focus on quality assurance, we partner with reputable manufacturers and brands globally to ensure that our customers receive nothing but the best.

Driven by a passion for customer satisfaction, we strive to exceed expectations by offering an unparalleled level of service, reliability, and flexibility. Our experienced team is dedicated to understanding the unique requirements of each client and delivering tailored solutions that meet their specific needs efficiently and effectively.
